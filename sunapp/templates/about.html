About Poetry 4 the Park

Poetry 4 the Park is an interactive popup website. Art portals are created around the world, and when you find one, you are invited to submit your poetry, art, and images, and to view the work of others! It doesn't even need to rhyme, it just needs to be from the heart! Keep it anonymous, or link to your social websites to meet other artists in your area!
