About Gravity Cult

Gravity Cult is an interactive popup website. Art portals are created around the world, and when you find one, you are invited to submit your poetry, art, and images, and to view the work of others! It doesn't even need to rhyme, it just needs to be from the heart! Keep it anonymous, or link to your social websites to meet other artists in your area and around the world!

We believe that sharing art and culture is one of the highest callings of humanity. We hope to create a global, digital, outdoor community of people who wish to use the Internet in the spirit of artistic endeavor and self-expression!
